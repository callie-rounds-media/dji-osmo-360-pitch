Idea 01 Location · Boituva, Brazil

Helicopter Skydiving in Brazil

The Concept

Lift off in a helicopter from Boituva, climbing over the deep green of the Brazilian jungle as it unfolds in every direction. Reach altitude over endless canopy, then step out into open air for a full skydive descent. Osmo 360 in hand the whole way, every direction captured from a single mount.

Idea 02 Location · Ölüdeniz, Turkey

Paramotor Skydiving in the Mediterranean

The Concept

Launch a paramotor off Babadağ Mountain into the Turkish Mediterranean sky, one of the world's top three free-flight destinations. At altitude, separate from the wing for a free skydive descent back to the coast at Ölüdeniz. Osmo 360 in hand to capture it all.

Hot Air Balloon Skydiving in Boituva
